Welcome to Snake Game, a delightful game crafted by a passionate Bachelor of Technology Computer Science Engineering student with a love for coding and creativity!

With vibrant graphics and engaging gameplay, Snake Game promises hours of fun for players of all ages.

  • Compete with friends and family.
  • Be aware of turns count.
